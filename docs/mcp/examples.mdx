## Daily Planning

> **You:** "What do I have scheduled for today?"

The AI calls `list_blocks` with today's date and presents your schedule.

> **You:** "I want to add a Math study session from 2pm to 3:30pm about Linear Algebra"

The AI calls `create_block` with the subject, time, and topic.

---

## Weekly Review

> **You:** "How was my study week? Give me a summary."

The AI calls `get_stats` with `period: "week"` and formats a summary:

```
Weekly Summary:
- 35/42 blocks completed (83%)
- 35 hours studied
- 7-day streak!
- Top subject: JavaScript (12 blocks)
- Needs attention: Mathematics (only 3/8 completed)
```

---

## Schedule Optimization

> **You:** "I want to focus more on Math next week. I have 4 hours per day, mornings are best."

The AI calls `suggest_schedule` and presents recommendations:

```
Suggested changes:
1. Mathematics: Mon-Fri 08:00-09:30 (up from 2 days)
2. JavaScript: Mon/Wed/Fri 10:00-10:45 (reduced)
3. Morning Routine: Keep daily 07:00-07:30

Would you like me to apply these changes?
```

## Quick Actions

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Mark done">
    "Mark my 9am JavaScript block as done"

    → `update_block({ block_id, done: true })`
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reschedule">
    "Move my math block to 3pm"

    → `update_block({ block_id, start: "15:00", end: "16:00" })`
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cancel block">
    "Delete my evening study block"

    → `delete_block({ block_id })`
  </Card>
  <Card title="New activity">
    "Create a Physics study profile with MWF at 11am"

    → `create_subject({ name, type, slots })`
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Advanced: Building Automations

You can combine MCP tools with your AI assistant's capabilities:

> **You:** "Every Monday, check my previous week's completion rate. If it's below 80%, suggest adjustments for the new week."

The AI can chain:
1. `get_stats({ period: "week" })` — check last week
2. If `completion_rate < 0.8` → `suggest_schedule()` — get suggestions
3. Present recommendations for your approval

<Info>
  The MCP Server doesn't auto-apply changes. Your AI always asks for confirmation before modifying your schedule.
</Info>
