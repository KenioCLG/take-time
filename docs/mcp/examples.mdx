## Daily Planning

> **You:** "What do I have scheduled for today?"

The AI calls `list_blocks` with today's date and presents your schedule.

> **You:** "I want to add a Math study session from 2pm to 3:30pm about Linear Algebra"

The AI calls `create_block` with the subject name, time, and topic.

---

## Weekly Review

> **You:** "How was my study week? Give me a summary."

The AI calls `get_stats` with `period: "week"` and formats a summary:

```
Weekly Summary:
- 35/42 blocks completed (83%)
- 35 hours studied
- 7-day streak!
- Top subject: JavaScript (12 blocks)
- Needs attention: Mathematics (only 3/8 completed)
```

## Smart Combinations

Your AI can chain tools for complex requests:

> **You:** "Check my completion rate for this week. If any subject is below 50%, create extra blocks for it tomorrow."

The AI chains:
1. `get_stats({ period: "week" })` — check completion
2. `list_subjects()` — get all activities
3. `create_block(...)` — schedule make-up blocks for struggling subjects

> **You:** "I'm done studying for today. Mark all remaining blocks as done."

The AI chains:
1. `list_blocks({ date: "2026-06-11", done: false })` — find pending blocks
2. `update_block({ block_id, done: true })` — mark each one

<Info>
  The MCP Server doesn't auto-apply changes. Your AI always asks for confirmation before modifying your schedule.
</Info>
